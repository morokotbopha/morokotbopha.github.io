Essentials

Essential are consider to be the fundamental of Liger's educations. Every student including me takes four essential subjects every term which include English literacy, Khmer literacy, math, and science. Our teacher are called "Facilitator". They work alongside students to identify problem and design a solution to address the problem. To me, this shows another side of education. I've learned that education isn't just about knowledge, memorizing, writting notes and doing exercise. It is rather about fufilling all of the curiosity and gaining skills that can be used on the campus and beyond. On top of that, I do not only sit on the chair to listen to the facilitator but I interact with other students and ask questions.
