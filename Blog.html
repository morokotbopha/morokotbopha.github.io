9th e-Icon | Experience

During August 2019 I attended the 9th e-ICON world contest held in busan, korea. The contest are made up of many participant from different places. Competitive would never suitable to describe the contest due to the fact that I always feel relaxed by how other participant communicate with other without the eagerness to win over the other. The contest require us all to create an application to solve problem in society. The problem My team has chosen was about disability. During of developing the software itself, one of my korean temmate and I went off to interview a disbility communicity about their experiences living with disability. We won the 2nd place among all of the teams from different countries. The teamwork and experiences of the contest remind me that knowing new people, learning different cultures and helping to solve problem in the society will be another memorable experience added to my list.
